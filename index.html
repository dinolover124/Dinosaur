Dinosaurs

Dinosaurs are prehistoric animals that lived from 252 until 65 million years ago. Scientists still dont understand how they were extinct. Some say that they were extinct from a meteor shower, some say it was some kind of global warming.

    • Dinosaur Timeline Areas

    I know alot of you have heard about dinosaurs, but do you really know about their Era/Timeline of existence?

    1. The Triassic Period

    The Triassic Period, the first timeline area for dinosaurs. It began from 252 million years ago until 199 million years ago. Dinosaurs that lived in that timeline are carnivores, herbivores, and omnivores such as Staurikosaurus, Postosuchus, Plateosaurus, and small omnivore Coelophysis.

    2. The Jurassic Period

    The Jurassic Period, the middle timeline area for the dinosaurs, some call it the golden age of the dinosaurs. It began from 199 million years ago until 145 million years ago. Dinosaurs that lived in that timeline are ferocious predators such as Allosaurus, and some herbivores such as Stegosaurus and Diplodocus

    3. The Cretaceous Period

    The Cretaceous Period, The last timeline area for dinosaurs. It began in 145 million years ago, but sadly it ended the Mesozoic Era in 65 million years ago. Some dinosaurs that lived in that period are some of the most recognizable dinosaur of all, The Tyrannosaurus Rex, Spinosaurus, Corythosaurus, and Velociraptor.

    Dinosaur Species

    You all have probably seen the different species of dinosaurs in books, or youtube, or movies, but i dont think you know that the real species arent in the names.

    1. Sauropods

    Sauropods are the biggest herbivores of the dinosaurs, like Apatosaurus, Brachiosaur, Camarasaurus. They all have things in common, Long Necks, long tails, small head and four thick pillar like legs.

    2. Theropods

    Theropods are carnivores, like The Tyrannosaurus Rex, Spinosaurus, Deinonychus. They also have things in common like how they walk on two feet, they also have three toe'd limbs.

    3. Ornithischians

    Ornithischians are herbivores that dont have long necks or long tails, like Triceratops, Stegosaurus, Parasaurolophus. They are herbivores that can defend themselves when needed.

    Top 3 Most Popular Dinosaurs

    1. Tyrannosaurus

    Tyrannosaurus is a genus of coelurosaurian theropod dinosaur. The species Tyrannosaurus rex (rex meaning "king" in Latin), often called T. rex or colloquially T-Rex, is one of the most well-represented of the large theropods. Tyrannosaurus lived throughout what is now western North America.

    2. Triceratops

    Triceratops is a genus of herbivorous ceratopsid dinosaur that first appeared during the late Cretaceous period, about 68 million years ago in what is now North America. It is one of the last known non-avian dinosaur generation, and became extinct in the Cretaceous extinction event 66 million years ago. The name Triceratops, which literally means "three-horned face".

    3. Stegosaurus

    Stegosaurus, is a genus of herbivorous thyreophoran dinosaur. Fossils of this genus date to the Late Jurassic period, where they are found in Kimmeridgian to early Tithonian aged strata, between 155 and 150 million years ago, in the western United States and Portugal. It's name means "Roofed Lizard".
